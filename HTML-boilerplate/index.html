The Dog in Kivulu

His name is Simba. A bold and epic fighter, leader of the pack, an alpha male. Simba enjoys his job, and is proud to always tell off rival packs.

Simba's Legacy.

For his bravery, puppy has over 10 bitches to his name, and over 50 puppies born to his legacy. He is now old and shaggy but the proud look on his face is very telling of a life well lived.

Long Simba
